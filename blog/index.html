Thought on Artificial Intelligence

May 10, 2014 - Originally written on October 14, 2010

The essential difference between humans and computers is that the way of reasoning itself is a subject of observation for humans.

Reasoning is an inborn ability for humans. All humans do reasoning though it is not clear what reasoning is. No one grasps the rules of reasoning completely for now. If there were such people, all problems are just calculations for them. Only clues to grasp the rules of reasoning that we can get are reasoning we did. Humans can gather clues from their reasoning and deepen the understanding of reasoning. It enables humans to get new ideas that they never imagined.

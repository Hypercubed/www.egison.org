Pattern-matching-oriented

Egison proposes a new paradigm pattern-matching-oriented. Following features enables intuitive powerful pattern-matching.

  • Modularization of the way of pattern-matching
  • Multiple pattern-matching results
  • Non-linear patterns
  • Lexical scoping of patterns
